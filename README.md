 ## Project Description
 This project focuses on analyzing **corporate sales data** to understand business performance, customer segmentation, product demand, and regional insights.

The dashboard uses **interactive Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts** in Excel to explore metrics such as:

- Total Sales, Total Orders, Total Profit, Profit Margin
- Sales by Category, Sub-Category, Segment, Region
- Monthly Sales Trends
- Profit Percentage by Segment
- Delivery Performance & Discounts

## Dashboard Insights
- **Technology** generates the highest sales and profit.  
- **West** region performs best with the highest revenue.  
- **Consumer segment** contributes the largest share of profit.  
- Top-performing sub-categories: **Phones, Chairs, Binders**. 
- Monthly sales peaks occur in **October, November, December**.
- Sub-categories like **Tables and Bookcases** show lower profitability.

---

## Recommendations
The Excel dashboard includes:
1. **Invest more in high-performing categories** like Technology.
2. **Reduce heavy discounts** in low-profit segments to increase margin.
3. **Expand marketing in underperforming regions** (e.g., South).
4. **Strengthen inventory** for high-demand sub-categories (Phones, Chairs).
5. **Optimize supply chain** to reduce delivery time below 4 days.
